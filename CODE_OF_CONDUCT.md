# Code of Conduct

Kelompok is built for communities, nonprofits, and public-interest organizations. The project should be a welcoming place for contributors with different backgrounds, skill levels, languages, and lived experiences.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive
- Assume good intent while still addressing harm clearly
- Give feedback on ideas and code, not personal traits
- Keep discussions focused on the project and its users
- Make space for beginners and non-native English speakers
- Handle sensitive organization and community data with care

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, or personal attacks
- Discriminatory language or behavior
- Sharing private information without permission
- Dismissing contributors through insults or mockery
- Repeatedly derailing project discussions
- Publishing secrets, credentials, or sensitive data

## Reporting

This project is not publicly launched yet.

Until a public reporting channel is created, report conduct concerns privately to the maintainers.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove comments, close issues, reject contributions, or restrict participation when behavior harms the project or its community.
